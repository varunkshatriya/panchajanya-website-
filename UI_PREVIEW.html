Company Overview

Our Journey

Founded in 2009, Panchajanya Pet Care started with a vision to create premium pet care products that retailers and distributors could trust. What began as a small manufacturing unit has grown into a trusted partner for over 5,000 retailers across India.

  • Started with 5 product lines
  • Now manufacturing 100+ SKUs
  • GMP & ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • Serving 500,000+ pets annually
